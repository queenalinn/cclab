Scientific Name

Hydroblurbus Aquatica

Discovered By / At

Discovered by aquatic scientics in Bora Bora in 2003

Family

It is closely related to the cuttlefish and distancely related to polar bears.

Habitat

Blurby can be found on the beaches of Bora Bora surrounded by colorful fishes and beautiful color changing seashells.

Appearance

Blurby has 5 short tentcales that are chubby and smooth. It has a large body that is bright pink! It has big twinkle eyes with a small smile. It has has bear ears that allow them to hear prededators approaches. They are very small only the size of a golf ball.

Key Characteristics

Loves to play with fishes

Best friends with crabs

Eats starfishes

It lives in the coral reef of the blue BLUE ocean. When Blurby is tickled it blows out bubbles. It loves to play with you so it will chase you around!
