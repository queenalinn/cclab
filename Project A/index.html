Deep In the SeaBlurb

Scientific Name:

Hydroblurbus Aquatica

Discovered by / at:

Discovered by American scientists in Bora Bora, living in the deep blue ocean.

Family:

Blurby's family, known as the Blurbians, inhabited the deep blue ocean, where they had thrived for generations, unnoticed by human eyes. They are a fascinating blend of cuttlefish and polar bear ancestry, resulting in a unique and endanger species.

Habitat:

Blurby can be found in the blue ocean, that blows bubbles every second of the day forever. Above land tehy are surrounded by different color and size.

Appearance:

Blurby has 5 short tentcales that are chubby and smooth. It has a round body that is bright pink! It has big twinkle eyes with a small smile. It has has bear ears that allow them to hear prededators that may approach them. They are pretty small, around the size of a small crab.

Key Characteristics:

  • Best friends with crabs
  • Can maniputlate seashell colors
  • Ability to summon bright color fish
  • Allergic to dark colors

What we know so far about Blurby? Blurby is a cute creature residing in the depths of the blue ocean, being related to both cuttlefish and distant polar bears. Blurby can summon fish and enjoys playful interactions with crabs. His unique talent is the ability to manipulate the size and color of seashells everyday, showcasing both his creativity and adaptability. Blurby is part of a species called the Blurbians, who inhabit underwater caves illuminated by bioluminescent algae.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Blurby is always a curious creature it loves to see what you are doing so their eyes' always follow you around. You can also feed Blurby with fish by using mouse click. Additionally, you can also press "b" for Blurby to change the color of the sand!
